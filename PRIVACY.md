# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 7, 2026

The website **zzzzshawn.cloud** is a personal portfolio created and maintained by Shawn (“I”, “me”, or “my”). I respect your privacy and am committed to handling your data in a transparent, minimal, and secure way.

When you visit this website, some data may be collected to operate the site, understand how it is used, and improve your experience. This document explains what is collected, how it is used, and what rights you may have.

## 1. Data I Collect

### 1.1. Information You Provide Directly

Depending on how you interact with the site, you may choose to share certain information with me, for example:

- **Contact information** when you email me (such as your email address and the content of your message).
- **Other information** that you voluntarily provide when you communicate with me via channels linked on the site (e.g. GitHub, X/Twitter, LinkedIn, Discord).

This information is used only to respond to you and maintain communication. It is not used for mass advertising or sold to third parties.

### 1.2. Usage Data

When you access **zzzzshawn.cloud**, the system may automatically collect certain technical and usage data, for example:

- **Vercel Analytics** (see Section 2.1): anonymous, aggregated data such as page views, referrer, country/region/city, device type, browser, and operating system. This data is not tied to your identity or IP address; session data is discarded after 24 hours.
- **Hosting logs**: The hosting provider (Vercel) may log IP addresses and HTTP request data for operational, security, and performance purposes.

I do not attempt to link usage data to your real-world identity.

### 1.3. Local Storage and Similar Technologies

The website may use:

- **Local storage** for strictly necessary or functional purposes, such as saving your **theme preference** (light/dark mode) so the site works as you expect on return visits.
- **No third-party advertising or tracking cookies** are used by this site.

## 2. Third-Party Tools and Services

### 2.1. Vercel (Hosting and Analytics)

The site is built and hosted on **Vercel**. Vercel provides:

- **Hosting and CDN**: Vercel may log IP addresses and HTTP request information for operational, security, and performance monitoring.
- **Vercel Web Analytics**: A privacy-focused analytics product that:
  - Collects only anonymous, aggregated data (e.g. page views, referrer, country, device type, browser).
  - Does **not** use cookies to track you across sites.
  - Does **not** collect data that can identify you personally; session data is automatically discarded after 24 hours.

For more information, see [Vercel’s documentation on Web Analytics and privacy](https://vercel.com/docs/analytics).

### 2.2. Optional: NASA APOD API

The site may optionally display an “Astronomy Picture of the Day” image from **NASA’s APOD API**. When that feature is used, the site sends a request to NASA’s servers (with no personal data about you). NASA’s use of request data is governed by [NASA’s privacy policy](https://www.nasa.gov/privacy/).

### 2.3. Fonts and Assets

- **Fonts**: The site uses Google Fonts (e.g. Space Grotesk) loaded through Next.js font optimization. When your browser loads these resources, the relevant provider may see your IP address. Font files may be served by Vercel’s infrastructure depending on configuration.
- **Other assets** (images, icons) are primarily served from this site’s own domain or the same hosting infrastructure.

Each third-party provider has its own privacy practices. Data handled by them is subject to their respective privacy policies in addition to this document.

## 3. How I Use Your Data

I use the data described above for the following purposes:

- **Operating the website**: to ensure the site functions correctly, is reliable, and is protected against abuse.
- **Analytics and improvement**: to understand which pages are visited and how the site performs, using only anonymous, aggregated data.
- **Communication with you**: when you contact me directly (e.g. via email or social links), I use the information you provide to respond to your inquiry.

I **do not** sell or rent your data for advertising or third-party marketing.
